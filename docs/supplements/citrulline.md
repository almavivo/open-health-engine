## Guidance

**Dose:** 6 g L-citrulline or 8 g citrulline malate, 30–60 minutes pre-training.

**Timing:** Pre-training; map to morning window for the schedule model.

**Reassessment:** Reassess after 2 to 4 weeks.

## Rationale

- Improves training volume (reps to failure) and reduces post-exercise soreness in trained populations.
- Effect on power output is smaller than the marketing implies.
