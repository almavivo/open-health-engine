## Guidance

**Dose:** Acute use under cognitive stress; trials use 100–150 mg/kg.

**Timing:** Morning or 30–60 minutes before a high-demand task.

**Reassessment:** Use as needed; not daily.

## Rationale

- Real signal for cognition under acute stress, sleep loss, or cold; not a chronic supplement.
- Less useful in well-rested, low-stress conditions.
