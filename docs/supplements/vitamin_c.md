## Guidance

**Dose:** 200–500 mg daily; higher doses don't proportionally help and can cause GI upset.

**Timing:** Morning with food; pair with iron supplementation for absorption.

**Reassessment:** Reassess after 4 to 8 weeks.

## Rationale

- Real but modest signal for cold duration when started early; little benefit if started after symptoms peak.
- Useful pairing with iron supplementation; otherwise diet usually covers it.
