## Guidance

**Dose:** 3.2–6.4 g daily, ideally split into 2–4 smaller doses to reduce tingling.

**Timing:** Morning or pre-training; daily loading matters more than acute timing.

**Reassessment:** Reassess after 4 to 6 weeks.

## Rationale

- Best evidence is for high-intensity work lasting 1–4 minutes (rowing, intervals, mid-distance).
- Acts via muscle carnosine; takes weeks to load.
