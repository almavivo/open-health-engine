## Guidance

**Dose:** 150 mcg daily for adequacy. Stay below 1100 mcg/day to avoid thyroid disturbance.

**Timing:** Morning.

**Reassessment:** Reassess after 8 to 12 weeks; consider TSH check.

## Rationale

- Most useful for non-iodized-salt households, certain restrictive diets, and pregnancy.
- Excess iodine can trigger thyroid dysfunction; not a 'more is better' nutrient.
