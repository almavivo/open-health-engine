## Guidance

**Dose:** 40–80 mg total isoflavones daily.

**Timing:** Morning.

**Reassessment:** Reassess after 8 to 12 weeks.

## Rationale

- Modest hot-flash reduction in some menopause trials.
- Effects vary widely by genetics (equol-producer status).
