## Guidance

**Dose:** Most adults shouldn't supplement preformed vitamin A. RDA can usually be hit by diet.

**Timing:** Morning with fat-containing meal.

**Reassessment:** Reassess after 12 weeks.

## Rationale

- Most users don't need this; teratogenic and hepatotoxic at chronic high doses.
- Beta-carotene supplementation in smokers raised lung cancer risk in trials — not a safe blanket recommendation.

## Why not in the core stack

- Limited safety data
