## Guidance

**Dose:** 500–600 mg per dose, max 1000 mg/day total from supplements (food + supplement).

**Timing:** Evening with food; split into two doses if total exceeds 500 mg.

**Reassessment:** Reassess after 12 weeks.

## Rationale

- Most useful for low-dairy diets, post-menopausal women, and older adults at fracture risk.
- Excess supplementation has been associated with cardiovascular concerns; food sources first.
