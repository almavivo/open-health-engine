## Guidance

**Dose:** 1–2 mg daily; pair with zinc supplementation to prevent depletion.

**Timing:** Morning, separated from zinc by 2 hours if both are taken.

**Reassessment:** Reassess after 12 weeks.

## Rationale

- Easily depleted by long-term high-dose zinc supplementation.
- Deficiency presents as anemia not responsive to iron.
