## Guidance

**Dose:** RDA is 15 mg/day. Avoid chronic doses above 268 mg/day.

**Timing:** Morning with fat-containing meal.

**Reassessment:** Reassess after 12 weeks.

## Rationale

- Most users get enough from diet; bleeding risk and mixed trial outcomes argue against routine supplementation.
- Narrow case in NAFLD where higher doses are sometimes used under clinician care.

## Why not in the core stack

- Smaller effect size
- Limited safety data
