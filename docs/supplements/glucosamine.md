## Guidance

**Dose:** 1500 mg daily (commonly as a single morning dose).

**Timing:** Morning with food.

**Reassessment:** Reassess after 8 to 12 weeks.

## Rationale

- Mixed evidence overall; strongest signal in moderate knee OA with crystalline glucosamine sulfate.
- Often paired with chondroitin in trials.

## Why not in the core stack

- Smaller effect size
