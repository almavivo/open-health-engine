## Guidance

**Dose:** Match sodium to sweat losses; 500–1500 mg sodium per hour during prolonged exercise or sauna.

**Timing:** During or around exercise; or once daily if low-carb (which depletes sodium).

**Reassessment:** Reassess after 2 to 4 weeks.

## Rationale

- Useful for endurance training, hot environments, sauna users, and low-carb dieters who lose sodium fast.
- Most products under-dose sodium; the fancy minerals are usually marketing.
