## Guidance

**Dose:** Standardized to monacolin K; treat exactly as a low-dose statin.

**Timing:** Evening with food.

**Reassessment:** Reassess with lipid panel after 8 to 12 weeks.

## Rationale

- Effective LDL reduction because it contains naturally-occurring lovastatin (monacolin K).
- EU has restricted potency; quality and safety vary widely outside regulated markets.

## Why not in the core stack

- Limited safety data

- **History of liver problems** is **Yes** or **Not sure**
- **Are you currently taking a statin (e.g. atorvastatin, rosuvastatin, simvastatin)?** is **Yes**
