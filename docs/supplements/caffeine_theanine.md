## Guidance

**Dose:** About 1:2 caffeine to L-theanine (e.g. 100 mg caffeine + 200 mg L-theanine).

**Timing:** Morning only; cut caffeine 8–10 hours before bed.

**Reassessment:** Reassess after 1 to 2 weeks.

## Rationale

- Among the better-evidenced cognitive pairings: caffeine for activation, L-theanine to soften jitter.
- If you don't already have caffeine timing dialed in, fix that before adding nootropics.
