# Contributors

This file lists everyone who has contributed substantively to
`@almavivo/engine`. Every merged contribution earns a credit line
here with name, credential, and (optional) practice or website
link.

The same list is mirrored on the
[Almavivo Trusted Reviewers page](https://almavivo.com/trusted-reviewers)
once the project has its first cohort of named contributors.

## How to be listed

Submit a PR using either the standard code-change template or the
[Clinician Review template](.github/PULL_REQUEST_TEMPLATE/clinician_review.md).
At the bottom of either template you will find a "Reviewer credit"
section asking how you would like to be listed. We honour:

- The exact spelling of your name
- Your credential (MD, PharmD, RDN, ND, MS, PhD, etc.)
- A single optional URL — your practice, your university page, a
  professional profile, your own site
- Or "anonymous" — if you would prefer not to be named, we won't
  list you, but the contribution still merges

## What counts as substantive

Any of the following merits a credit line:

- A new supplement rule with a complete evidence package
- A new lab specification with guideline anchor
- A new derived signal with confounder analysis
- A safety-guard fix (excludeIf / clinicianReviewIf)
- A correction to a citation (especially with a refreshed
  `lastReviewed` date)
- An addition to `EXCLUDED_SUPPLEMENTS` or `EXCLUDED_TESTS` with
  cited reasoning
- A new property-based invariant test
- A clinically meaningful question added to the questionnaire

Pure typo fixes, formatting changes, dependency bumps, and CI
tweaks are appreciated and merged but do not generate a credit
line — they live in git history.

## Conflict of interest

Contributors with financial relationships to supplement brands,
lab companies, or advocacy organisations are welcome — those
contributions are often the most substantive. The relationship
must be disclosed in the PR (a field exists in the Clinician
Review template). Reviewers weigh contributions accordingly. The
disclosure is also reflected here as a notation next to the
contributor's credit line where relevant.
